AstroClipz

A personal publishing tool for turning livestream moments into short-form clips — reviewed, edited and approved by a human before anything is posted.

How it works

  • Capture

    The tool watches livestreams that AstroClipz has written permission to clip and flags high-engagement moments.

  • Review

    Every clip lands in a private review queue. The operator trims it, edits the title, captions and subtitles, and decides whether it should exist at all.

  • Approve

    Nothing is posted automatically. Each clip is individually approved before it is uploaded.

  • Publish

    Approved clips are uploaded to AstroClipz's own social media accounts using the platforms' official APIs.

Scope of this tool: AstroClipz is operated by its developer to publish to accounts the developer owns and has connected. It is not offered as a product or service to third parties, and it does not access other users' data.
